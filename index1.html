About Our shop

Grand Line Café

At Grand Line Coffee, we are dedicated to supporting Filipino coffee farmers by sourcing high-quality local beans. Our collective technical expertise allows us to create a product that competes with the biggest names in the industry while remaining affordable for every Filipino. With our exceptional coffee and modern brand marketing, we aim to make a great cup of coffee accessible to anyone, anywhere, at any time.

About

Established in 2024, Grand Line Cafe started as a small neighborhood spot with a passion for quality coffee and community. Over the years, we’ve grown into a beloved gathering place where locals and travelers alike can enjoy artisanal coffee, homemade pastries, and a welcoming atmosphere. Inspired by the journey of discovery, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots.
